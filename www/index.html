#NAMETAG DAY!

Wear a nametag, make a friend!

June 1, NYC / May 31, San Francisco

Imagine this: on June 1, everyone in New York City will wear a nametag. We're going to set up distribution sites around the city, and we'll need all the help we can get!

Ever wanted to connect with the millions of New Yorkers walking past you? Each day brings opportunities to make new friends and share experiences. And yet, all too often, we can forget to notice the people around us. Nametag day aims to break this barrier and strengthen the human element of the New York experience, adding a bit of spontaneity and silliness to people's days.

There's no catch. We're not political. We are not selling anything. And we're radically participatory, which means that anyone and everyone can volunteer with us. Sign up on the site to find out how you can be a part of it.

If you've got something to add – a performance, art project, event, or any other creative endeavor – get in touch!
